1.5 Describing copyright and other constraints affecting websites

Copyright is protection on someone’s intellectual creation, be it written or an image (film/photo/drawing) & this extends to a website. Copyright and other intellectual property laws affect websites as they can restrict what content you are able to publish to the webpage. When using content from another source you would have to get permission from the original author or link to the original with permission from the original author. The content in a webpage was separate copyrights affecting it ranging from literacy copyright for text and code, images would be artistic works and music can be protected by sound recording copyrights. These are all things I will have to consider for my own website.
